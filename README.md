## ğŸ“– How to Use

### Saving Favorites

Just click the â¤ï¸ heart icon on any furniture you like (or press `F` when viewing in the lightbox). It'll be saved to your favorites. Then you can click "Favorites only" to see just your saved items.

### Creating Collections

Go to your Dashboard (click "Dashboard" in the top menu), then click "My Collections" in the sidebar. Hit "Create Collection", give it a name (description is optional), choose if it should be public or private, and create it. Then just browse furniture and click "Add to Collection" to add items to it.

### Sharing Collections

Go to "My Collections" in your dashboard, find the collection you want to share, make sure it's set to "Public", then click "Share" to copy the link. Anyone with the link can view your collection, even without logging in.

### Submitting Furniture

Go to your Dashboard, click "Submissions" in the sidebar, then "Submit New Furniture". Fill in the details (name, category, price if you know it, image URL and tags), then submit. Submissions are reviewed and added to the catalog if everything looks good!

### Suggesting Edits

Find the furniture item, click the image to open the lightbox, then click "Suggest Edit". Make your changes and submit, it'll be reviewed and updated if the changes are correct.

### Exporting Commands

**Export Favorites:** Go to Dashboard â†’ "My Favorites", click "Export Commands", then choose to copy to clipboard or download as a text file.

**Export Collection:** Go to Dashboard â†’ "My Collections", open the collection, click "Export Commands", then copy or download.
